PocketProject

Small ideas. Real builds. Learn by making things.

What is PocketProject?

PocketProject is a YSWS where you build small, niche, experimental or unexpectedly useful projects — hardware or software. Not everything has to change the world. Just build something and learn from it.

What can you make?

tiny tools weird websites hardware gadgets automations game mods experimental apps

Goal

Learn something new, build something real, and ship it — even if it’s messy.

Reward idea

Hardware kits, SBCs, electronics, dev tools, and stuff that helps your next project.
